Baby Trainers' Plan to Enlighten Babies Everywhere

By: Zijinhui, Max and Rafid

What are our goals?

Babies are the past, present, and future. Literally. We were all once babies in the past, we as a race need to have babies in the present to survive, and lastly, the future will be run by said present day babies. That got the folks at Baby Trainers thinking...

Babies: Untapped Liquid Gold

Baby Trainer's aim to use the untapped resource of babies and their extreme ability of memorization to allow the rapid fast memorization of coding languages. If looked at in an emotionless, inhumane way, babies are the perfect candidates to create absolute GENII in software development, which will in turn revolutionize the modern day world. With such an influx of advanced, prodigy level babies, the online infrastructure of the world will face unprecedented amounts of technological advances in a very short period of time (By the time the babies can actually speak and stand up on their own).

So How DO We Train Your Baby?

The procedure that Baby Trainers implement in order to have your baby learn the secrets of the coding world is very simple. After the immediate birth of the infant, our certified and vetted baby trainer will remove the baby from its mother's loving touch and instantly enroll them into a harsh 6 hour per day, 5 days per week bootcamp distributed by TTP (Tech Talent Pipeline) teaching them the basics of Git, Github, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and React until they are able to speak and cry for food (or help!). This advanced and intuitive camp is crafted by some of the most intelligent instructors and have extremely friendly TA's that will assist your baby's needs to ensure as smooth of a process as possible.

How Do We Use Donations?

Monetary Donations

For all monetary donations, we use a staggering 2% of the funds towards allowing these babies to learn software development skills. The rest of the funds go towards legal fee's and lawyers, as apparently the operations we carry out are vulnerable to allegations of "kidnapping" and "illegitimacy".

Baby Donations

When a user donates a baby, they are give up the rights to the baby, allowing the certified and vetted trainers from Baby Trainers to take them to a gathering hall in which they are subjected to countless hours of extreme educational content, teaching them all sorts of imperative software engineering skills that will allow them to break through the doors of the software engineering world and accomplish great things.

Note: We request all donated babies belong to the donator, as after a recent lawsuit, the court decided donating another individual's baby is illegal.
